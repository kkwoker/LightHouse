Potatoe Stew

Potatoe Stew

-Beef-

Boil 'em, mash 'em , put 'em in a stew

Toggle Hide Images

Ingredients

  • NEED IT
    2lb beef cubes
  • NEED IT
    4 potatoes, peeled and cut in wedges
  • NEED IT
    3 carrots, cut in chunks
  • NEED IT
    2 cloves garlic
  • NEED IT
    1 1/2 cups beef stock
  • NEED IT
    1/2 tsp salt and pepper
  • NEED IT
    2tbsp all-purpose flour
Clear

Directions

  1. Mix together the beef cubes, potatoe wedges, carrot chunks into the beef stock into a slow cooking pot
  2. Cover and cook on Low for 10 hours or until beef and vegetables are tender
  3. Add salt and pepper
  4. Increase heat to High, mix in flour and stir.
  5. Cook for 5 minutes or until thickened
  6. Serve.
